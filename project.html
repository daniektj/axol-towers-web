Why to invest through NFTs?

We are currently innovating a new way of Real Estate investment, creating this NFT Collection to allow access to 3,330 people without the need to sign physical documents thanks to a smart contract. With smart contracts we can have the addresses of your portfolios automatically that means that every time there are profits in the project we will automatically send these profits to your balance creating a secure passive income.

Where it’s gonna be build?

The first project is gonna be developed at Tijuana, Baja California Norte, Mexico

Why is this different from the traditional way of investing?

Not to mention the lush amounts of money needed to be able to invest in Real Estate on your own, let’s talk about the opportunities there are to find a property worthwhile in percentage profit... Investing as a community we can create incredible buildings with an exponential profit margin.

How much will I earn?
For this dynamic we are using rarity in our Axolotls to assign a percentage of profit in 3 groups wich come in different quantitites
  • Rare has an amount of 1,500 in stock and the mint price is 0.33 ETH
  • Legendary has an amount of 1,000 in stock and the mint price is 0.66 ETH.
  • Mythical has an amount of 830 in stock and the mint price is 1 ETH.

Example:
The profits were $10,390,000 USD, so it is distributed by percentages.
  • Rare will receive 40% = $4,156,000 USD, every holder will receive $831 USD.
  • Legendary will receive 40% = $4,156,000 USD, every holder will receive $1,039 USD.
  • Mythical will receive 20% = $2,078,000 USD, every holder will receive $2,078 USD.
Will we have to wait for the project to be fully founded to start?
Our first goal is to reach 20% sales of our collection, with this we can cover the cost of documentation necessary to close the deal with the construction company, thanks to this we can start operating with those in charge of pre-sales, which allow us to actively sustain the construction and gradually comply with the retribution of holders participation.
How can I Mint an NFT?
First you will need a wallet to store your cryptocurrencies, we recommend MetaMask

After installing the wallet you will need to buy Ethereum, in Metamask you can do it from the extension with a credit/debit card using the Buy option.
